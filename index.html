On 18 November 2021, PONTI will open its doors at Wolstraat 23, Antwerp. This new gallery project by Yoeri Vanlangendonck, David Vermeiren, Thor Salden and Beau Stollenwerk focuses entirely on contemporary art.
PONTI aims to be a sanctuary for young artists, regardless of the medium they use to express their artistic urges. PONTI refers to buldig bridges. Bridges between artist and public and between artist and public space.
